abstract: >- 
This paper proposes a structural-prior-guided compressive sensing framework for the joint reconstruction of building layouts and concealed targets in through-the-wall radar imaging scenarios.   By exploiting the structural characteristics of building layouts and the sparsity of target echoes, the proposed method enables simultaneous reconstruction of environmental structures and hidden targets.   Experimental results demonstrate improved reconstruction accuracy and robustness compared with existing approaches.
